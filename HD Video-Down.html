How to Download Videos

For YouTube:

  1. Copy the URL of the YouTube video you want to download
  2. Paste it in the YouTube tab above
  3. Click "Download" and choose your preferred quality

For Instagram/TikTok:

  1. Open the Instagram/TikTok app and find the video
